Baltic Startup Visas

Picture the Baltic States from North to South: Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania! Fancy a startup in a cool and welcoming place which is also part of the European Union, Euro Zone and Schengen areas?




There are a handful of countries these days that offer Visas to encourage and attract young idea startups to their countries. One important thing to bear in mind is the ease at which you can obtain a resident permit visa to get your startup running. While one can always choose countries such as the USA, Canada, Australia and so on, they might not be the most visa-friendly. The beautiful Baltic States stand-out with their ease of getting your business up and running within a very short period of time, thanks to their government's initiatives in streamlining the visa process. This article aims to get you started on the salient features of the different Baltic Startup visas and provide a quick summary which would help you bootstrap.

ESTONIA

Startup Estonia has an interactive website on startup visa that would help you to get started quickly in your journey to Estonia.

  • Why is Estonia a startup paradise? What is the value for startups?
  • Are you eligible for a visa? Find out instantly by answering some basic questions.
  • Check out a comprehensive list of Estonian startups here.
Key Features of the program:
  • Duration: The visa is initially given for a period of 1 year, which can be further extended by 6 months. If you have an investment of 65000 EUR, you can apply for a residence permit for entrepreneurship which may be issued up to 5 years.
  • Family: The entrepreneur may also bring their family. Your spouse may also work in Estonia.
  • Criteria: Innovative and scalable business like Airbnb or Uber, not restaurants or one-man consultancies.
  • Investment: No prior funding is required, applicant should just prove that he holds a minimum of 140 EUR for every month planned to spend in Estonia.
  • Financing: Nothing out-of-the-box, but can apply to the grant by Enterprise Estonia.
  • Application Fee: The evaluation of your application with the startup committee is not charged.
  • Visa Costs & Timeframe: Around 160-180 EUR, a visa can be issued in 30 days while a residence permit would take longer than 2 months.
  • Other Useful Links: e-residency4 - Establish your trusted location-independent EU company online.

Steps to apply for a visa:

  1. Fill out the qualification form
  2. Decision by committee with 10 days and receiving the letter
  3. Apply for the visa
  4. Let the adventure begin

LATVIA

Startup Latvia offers a comprehensive document on visa procedures to assist you in starting-up in Latvia easily.

  • Why Latvia? Check out 10 reasons why you should start your company in Latvia.
  • Explore some of the startups from Latvia here.

Key features of the program:

  • Duration: The visa is provided for a maximum duration of 3 years.
  • Family: The visa is spouse and children friendly.
  • Criteria: A sound startup idea described in 1 page, no business plans or qualification interviews.
  • Investment: Prior investment is not required. Bank statement to prove 12 months of subsistence (430 EUR/month) is adequate.
  • Financing: Apply through one of the schemes of ALTUM.
  • Team: One startup can have up to 5 team members with a startup visa.
  • Application Fee & Timeframe: There is a minimal cost of 100 EUR and the application would be processed in 30 days. Express processing is also possible in 10/5 working days for 200/400 EUR.
  • Visa Costs & Timeframe: A stamp duty of 14 EUR must be paid for the TRP card which would be processed in 10 working days. Express processing within 2 working days is possible for 28 EUR.
  • Other Useful Links: Labs of Latvia5

Steps to obtain your Startup Visa:

  1. Book an appointment in your nearest embassy
  2. Submit your application package
  3. Wait for the decision letter
  4. Order your temporary residence permit (TRP) card
  5. Pick up your card and move to Latvia

LITHUANIA

Startup Lithuania provides information on visas which will help you to transition your startup to Lithuania in a smooth manner.

  • Why should you consider Lithuania? Check out a quick video and also see this article.
  • Check your eligibility within a minute by answering a few questions

Key features of the program:

  • Duration: You initial visa is valid for 1 year, based on performance, you may extend it by 1 more year, beyond which general immigration requirements to be followed.
  • Family: Allowed to bring your family also for the duration of your visa.
  • Criteria: The startup must be in one of the following fields: Biotech, Nanotech, Lasertech, IT, Mechatronics or Electronics.
  • Investment: Prior investment is not mandatory. Apply with any source of funding as long as you can sustain for 1 year.
  • Financing: This program does not provide any financing, but will help in getting you in touch with VC's.
  • Application Fee: The application process is free of charge.
  • Visa Costs & Timeframe: Cost of obtaining a residence permit is between 114-228 EUR. The normal procedure takes 2 months, while expedited6 processing can be carried out in 1 month.

Steps to obtain your Startup Visa:

  1. Submit an application through the website, should include the overall strategy and a business pitch deck preferably
  2. Pitch your idea in an online video interview
  3. Evaluation Committee assessment (Represented by 40% public and 60% private sector experts)
  4. Receive official confirmation letter from Enterprise Lithuania7 & apply for the visa
  5. Move to Lithuania and start your business
