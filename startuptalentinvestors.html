Startup Talent Investors

FEATURED

WRITTEN BY XLYF ADMIN

Gone are the days of struggling through the entire process of starting a firm by carrying out all tasks alone such as thinking of an idea, finding a co-founder and working space, registering your company and contacting numerous venture capital companies day in-out to pitch your idea to finally obtain funding. The new kind of investors who fall under the category of 'Talent Investors' have setup pre-startup incubation programs who simply invest based on the talent of the person. One does not require an idea, company nor a co-founder, organisations like Antler1 and Entrepreneurs First2 take it a notch higher to help you get kickstarted by recruiting you for their highly selective 2 to 3 month programs with a generous stipend, working space, training, assistance with matching a co-founder and placing you in a panel where hundreds of investors are ready to invest in your brilliant ideas.

Let's first take a look at the newer of the two talent investors called Antler which was established in 2018 and is based out of Singapore. They have since spread out to 8 locations around the world | Singapore and Sydney in Asia Pacific | Amsterdam, London, Oslo & Stockholm in Europe | New York in North America | and Nairobi in Africa! The first phase (P1) of their program is typically 9-10 weeks during which they are given a stipend, candidates who further progress to the second phase (P2) of the program spend another 3-4 months.
