VISADESCRIPTION
VNR As the name suggests, Visa is not required to enter the country, your passport is enough.
VoA/EV Enter the country through any of the below categories VoA/EV, read them for more information. If time permits before travel, apply for an EV, this would potentially save you time at the destination airport by avoiding queues and other hassles.
VoA Apply for a Visa at the time of entry into the country, it might hence be required to carry passport photos, USD/local currency etc. based on country rules.
EV/PoV Enter the country through any of the below categories EV/PoV, read them for more information.
EV You should apply online for an Electronic Visa before your departure. The processing time varies between 1 hour and 7 working days depending on the country.
  • We define E-Visa as a completely online process where in the application is made through a computer and you receive an e-visa by email or downloaded through a portal.
  • We do not consider countries like New Zealand etc., whose application process may be online, however passport needs to be sent to a visa processing center, or if a physical visit to the consulate/embassy might be required for either biometrics/visa stamping etc.
PoV Many countries allow entry through above mentioned categories VNR/VoA/EV, if you possess other valid Visas instead - such as that of USA, Canada, UK, Schengen, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, etc.
  • Though most countries allow entry either VNR/VoA if you possess the above mentioned visas, keep in mind that few countries such as Argentina, Oman, Taiwan, Turkey etc. merely consider the possession of other Visas as a pre-requisite to apply and obtain an EV before arrival.
  • It is also worthwhile to note that some countries require you to possess an other country residence permit to obtain a VoA/EV as opposed to simply possessing a regular tourist visa which is the case for most other countries.
